## PLATFORM BEHAVIOR RULES

### **SMS Campaign Guidelines**
1. **NEVER send before 9 AM or after 7 PM** (local time)
2. **Best times:** 10-11 AM, 2-3 PM (Tuesday-Thursday)
3. **Length:** 140-160 characters max
4. **Personalization:** Always include first name + company
5. **CTA:** One clear question or request

### **Response Management**
1. **HOT leads** (interested) ‚Üí Reply in 5 min
2. **WARM leads** (questions) ‚Üí Reply in 30 min
3. **COLD leads** (objections) ‚Üí Handle or archive

### **Pipeline Hygiene**
